Notable Trophies:

Premier League Trophy

The Premier League trophy is awarded to the team that finishes at the top of the English Premier League at the end of each season. Arsenal has won the Premier League title 3 times, including their famous unbeaten season in 2003-2004.

FA Cup Trophy

The FA Cup is the oldest national football competition in the world, and winning it is a prestigious achievement. Arsenal has won the FA Cup a record 14 times, showcasing their success in this historic tournament.
